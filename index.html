Enjoy classic cars without the hassle

We are working on a concept to bring classic cars - and ownership - into the modern era.

We believe classic cars should be enjoyed, not tucked away or requiring constant tinkering. It should be sustainable, and affordable, whilst still being exhilarating. Electrification brings all that and more.

Here are some key aspects of our modern car share program currently in development:

We future-proof classic cars using modern electic drive trains and the latest battery technology

Unique ownership and membership model to appeal to owners and non-owners

Cars are shared by members through an app, whilst owners still have priority use

The cars are stored, maintained, cleaned and charged (by solar power) at the Charged Garage

Affordable, classic and all-electric

A modern car share program with a difference
